The Walled Garden Project

A Landmark Residence
in Rural Hertfordshire

Our client acquired a remarkable walled garden estate nestled within the rolling countryside of Hertfordshire. With sweeping views and layers of historic charm, the site sits within a registered park and garden, neighbouring a distinguished Grade II* listed property. The ambition: to create an extraordinary modern residence that reflects a refined lifestyle, respects the site's heritage, and sets a new benchmark for luxury living.

The Design

A Home of Two Worlds

The brief called for a home that blends refined family living with private leisure and wellness spaces. Designed as two distinct but interconnected parts, creating a seamless balance between daily function and exceptional indulgence.

Main Residence

A six-bedroom home with generous living, dining, kitchen and study areas designed for modern family life and entertaining at scale.

  • Six generously proportioned bedrooms
  • Open-plan living, dining and kitchen
  • Private study and library
  • Designed for entertaining at scale

Leisure Pavilion

A private retreat featuring world-class wellness and entertainment facilities, connected seamlessly to the main residence.

  • Indoor swimming pool
  • Gym and yoga studio
  • Private cinema
  • Below-ground luxury car collection
Sustainability

Legacy Beyond
Luxury

Designed to meet the RIBA 2030 Climate Challenge standards. Every decision measured against two principles: environmental sensitivity and timeless beauty.

Ultra-Low Energy
Photovoltaic panels and ground source heat pumps for minimal energy consumption
Low-Carbon Materials
Sustainably sourced charred timber, handmade brick, and reduced embodied carbon structural elements
Biodiverse Green Roof
Native wildflower seeding to support bird and insect habitats, reinforcing the home's connection to nature

Materiality & Presence

Sculptural yet Sympathetic

A warm and tactile material palette designed to sit quietly and confidently within the rural setting. Contemporary yet rooted in its surroundings.

Charred Timber

Depth, resilience, and a striking contemporary aesthetic that weathers beautifully over time

Handmade Brick

Grounding permanence at lower levels, sitting comfortably within the surrounding landscape

Green Roof

Softening visual presence and encouraging wildlife to flourish, connecting home to nature
